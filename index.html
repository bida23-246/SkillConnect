Why SkillExchange BW

Built for Botswana's education-to-employment gap.

Global platforms weren't built with Batswana students and graduates in mind. SkillExchange is — local payments, local institutions, local impact.

  • No international accounts needed — pay via Orange Money or MyZaka
  • Directly addresses Botswana's 43.9% youth unemployment rate
  • Portfolio system provides verifiable proof of skills for employers
  • Rating and review system maintains quality automatically
  • Aligned with LEA, CEDA and national youth empowerment goals
  • Browse for free — only pay when you're ready to connect
